Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 26, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to PadelBuzz. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy informs you about how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Data Controller

Application Name: PadelBuzz

Contact: contact@padelbuzz.pro

3. Data Collected

3.1 Registration Data

  • Email Address: Used to create and manage your account
  • Password: Stored in encrypted form to secure your account
  • First and Last Name: To personalize your profile

3.2 Profile Data

  • Profile Picture: Optional, to personalize your account
  • Date of Birth: To verify you meet the minimum age requirement
  • Phone Number: Optional, to facilitate contact between players

3.3 Location Data

  • Approximate Location: Used only if you authorize it, to help you find nearby padel courts
  • You can disable location access at any time in your device settings

3.4 Game Data

  • Match Videos: Recorded only if you activate the camera function during a match
  • Game Statistics: Match duration, scores, history
  • Application Settings: Notification preferences, video quality, etc.

3.5 Technical Data

  • Device Identifier: To ensure proper application functionality
  • Application Version: To provide you with appropriate updates
  • Error Logs: To improve application stability

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract Performance: To provide you with PadelBuzz services
  • Consent: For location, videos, and marketing communications
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve our service and ensure security

5. Use of Data

Your data is used to:

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Record and securely store your match videos
  • Generate automatic video highlights (premium feature)
  • Track your game statistics and history
  • Send you important notifications about the application
  • Improve our services and fix bugs
  • Ensure platform security

6. Data Sharing

We never sell your personal data. Your data may only be shared in the following cases:

6.1 With Your Consent

  • Other Players: Your name and profile picture are visible to other users
  • Video Sharing: Only if you choose to share your highlights

6.2 Service Providers

  • Supabase: Secure database hosting (GDPR-compliant servers in Europe)
  • Cloud Services: Encrypted video storage

6.3 Legal Obligations

  • If required by law or judicial authority

7. Data Retention

  • Active Account: Your data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Account: Your data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Videos: Retained until you manually delete them
  • Technical Logs: Retained for a maximum of 12 months

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Secure Authentication: Passwords hashed with modern algorithms
  • Restricted Access: Only authorized persons have access to data
  • Regular Backups: To prevent any data loss

9. Your Rights

In accordance with GDPR, you have the following rights:

9.1 Right of Access

You can request a copy of all your personal data

9.2 Right of Rectification

You can correct your data from the application settings

9.3 Right to Erasure

You can delete your account and all your data at any time via Settings > Profile > Delete My Account

9.4 Right to Data Portability

You can request to receive your data in a structured format

9.5 Right to Object

You can object to the processing of your data for marketing purposes

9.6 Right to Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw your consent at any time for location, notifications, etc.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: contact@padelbuzz.pro

10. Children's Data

PadelBuzz is intended for users aged 13 and over. If we discover that a user is under 13, we will immediately delete their account.

11. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our application does not use cookies. We only use:

  • Local Storage: To save your preferences and session
  • Device Identifiers: For proper application functionality

12. International Transfers

Your data is primarily stored on servers located in the European Union (Supabase). If a transfer outside the EU is necessary, we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. In case of significant changes, we will inform you via:

  • An in-app notification
  • An email to your registered address

The date of the last update is indicated at the top of this document.

14. Contact

For any questions regarding this privacy policy or your personal data:

Email: contact@padelbuzz.pro

From the app: Settings > Help > Contact Us

15. Supervisory Authority

If you believe your rights are not being respected, you can file a complaint with your local data protection authority or the CNIL (French Data Protection Authority):

  • Website: https://www.cnil.fr
  • Address: 3 Place de Fontenoy, 75007 Paris, France
