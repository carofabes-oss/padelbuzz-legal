Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Account and Login

Q: I forgot my password, how can I reset it?

A: On the login screen, tap "Forgot password" and follow the instructions sent to your email.

Q: How do I delete my account?

A: Go to Settings > Profile > Delete my account. Warning: this action is irreversible.

Q: Can I change my email address?

A: Contact us at contact@padelbuzz.app to update your email address.

Match Recording

Q: Why are my videos not recording?

A: Make sure that:

  • You have granted camera access in your phone settings
  • You have enough storage space
  • Your internet connection is stable for upload

Q: How long can I record?

A: Matches can last up to 3 hours. For longer durations, please contact support.

Q: Where are my videos stored?

A: Your videos are stored securely on our cloud servers. Only you can access your private videos.

Technical Issues

Q: The app crashes or stops responding

A: Try the following:

  1. Force-close the app and relaunch it
  2. Restart your phone
  3. Make sure you have the latest version of the app
  4. Reinstall the app (your data is saved in the cloud)

Q: The video quality is poor

A: Go to Settings > Video quality and select a higher quality. Note that this will use more data and storage.

Q: Notifications are not working

A: Make sure notifications are enabled in:

  • The app settings (Settings > Notifications)
  • Your phone settings (Settings > Notifications > PadelBuzz)

Privacy and Security

Q: Is my data secure?

A: Yes, we use encryption in transit and follow security best practices. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

Q: Can I download all my data?

A: Yes, contact us at contact@padelbuzz.app to receive a copy of all your personal data.

Q: Who can see my videos?

A: By default, only you can see your videos. You explicitly choose whether you want to share a highlight.

Report a Bug

If you encounter a bug or technical issue:

  1. Describe the problem: Explain what's not working
  2. Steps to reproduce: How did you encounter this issue?
  3. Device and version: Phone model, iOS version
  4. Screenshots: If possible, attach screenshots

Send this information to: contact@padelbuzz.app
